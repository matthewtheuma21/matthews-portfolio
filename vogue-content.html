my own creations of the VOGUE Posters

This project was initially to test my own skills using Photoshop, as it was a great way for me to practice these skills. Familiaried myself more with layout structuring, and knowing where and how material visually works in terms of hierachy communication. Then I tried filtering the images, manipulate and change the backgrounds to imploy a different setting and vibe from the original images.

Plus, it's super fun to see my friends' reactions when they see themselves on a Vogue cover! I have then even started doing these as Christmas presents. it's also a great addition to my digital design portfolio. It's a perfect example of blending creativity with technical skills. I honestly really like it.
