Comic Book - Beautiful Balloons

This project is a children’s comic book developed with a focus on visual storytelling and symbolic design. The concept uses a balloon as a recurring graphic element to represent emotional states, with changes in colour, scale, and movement guiding the narrative. Composition, pacing, and page layout were carefully considered to create a clear visual flow and maintain engagement across the sequence.

The design explores contrast with its vibrant colour palettes to emphasise emotional shifts, alongside consistent typography and imagery to create cohesion throughout the book. Particular attention was given to how each spread functions both individually and as part of a continuous reading experience. Although comic design is outside my usual focus, which is more aligned with advertising, textiles, and photography, this project challenged me to think differently about narrative through design. It strengthened my ability to communicate ideas visually and adapt my design approach to a new format.
