The Hidden Gems of Malta
My Own Website

WEBSITE LINK: https://matthewtheuma21.github.io/The-Hidden-Gems-in-the-Maltese-Islands/index.html

I wanted to spread the knowledge of our hidden beauty in Malta to people, Maltese and any tourists visiting Malta. However, I simply wanted to make the website better than any other website we find on the internet. Usually its either just reviews of the place (and not any official/valid information) like Travelodge, and some other websites are just one location per website. Or else its just a travel blog of a person who likes to travel. So why not create a useful website to easily find many different locations and beautiful spots in one website. I also added some of our best & most beautiful hotels here (maybe not the most affordable), but really recommended. I wanted to take in consideration the whole package when travelling in Malta.

I really like the colour combination of blue, yellow and white as a palette. It reminds you of a paradise, a sandy beach, imploying a touristic feeling. They make a good contrast.
