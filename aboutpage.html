I am currently in my final year at MCAST Mosta, completing a Bachelor of Arts (Hons) in Graphic Design. Over the course of my studies, I have developed a strong foundation across multiple design disciplines, working with a range of outputs and mediums including editorial, branding, photography, and digital design.

My approach to design is rooted in clarity, structure, and visual impact. I am particularly drawn to minimalism and the power of simplicity—often using limited colour palettes and carefully considered layouts to communicate ideas effectively. At the same time, I enjoy exploring more expressive and bold visual styles when the concept calls for it.

Throughout my work, I focus on creating designs that are both purposeful and engaging, with attention to composition, hierarchy, and narrative. My academic experience has pushed me to think more critically about design as a form of communication, not just aesthetics.

Beyond design, I value growth through new experiences and challenges. Travelling has played a significant role in shaping my perspective, allowing me to engage with different cultures and visual environments across Europe and the Americas. These experiences continue to influence and inform my creative approach.
