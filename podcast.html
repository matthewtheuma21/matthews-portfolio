GĦANDI BAGANDI ! - Very first
Podcast Idea

Presenting the very first idea that I had for a podcast. I always had a dream of creating a podcast on Spotfiy, discussing opinions and thoughts about various subjects.

Here's a little backstory...

The idea originated from a humorous moment shared with my best friend. We often joke and act silly together, and during one of our conversations in Maltese, she meant to say "Għandi basket roża mal-libsa" (meaning "I have a pink basket with my dress")—but instead, she accidentally blurted out "Għandi Bagandi!". We burst into laughter and couldn’t stop repeating it. The phrase became so iconic between us that I immediately thought it would make the perfect, quirky name for the podcast I had envisioned.

For context, "Għandi" means "I have" in Maltese. I built on this concept by structuring each podcast episode around a phrase starting with "Għandi", hinting at the episode’s theme or topic. I began working on visual branding for the podcast—designing Instagram posts, a profile icon, and creating a grungy, youthful aesthetic that suits the target audience I had in mind.

Although the podcast hasn’t launched yet, I’m still in search of the right partner to collaborate with. Ideally, it would have been my schoolmate - the girl featured beside me on the podcast cover. However, due to her busy schedule she couldn’t commit. So for now, it is just an idea, with a visual presentation.
