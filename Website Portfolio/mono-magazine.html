MAGAZINE MINI-PREVIEW

Presenting here, one of my most most favourite works I have done so far, as I have put so much dedication and time to it. It was only a school assignment, however I have planned photoshoots with a number of models that I know, been to places, planned everything according to all the themes and finding specific locations for each shoot (only the images of the celebrities are not taken by me). It was all a serious project. I took every step seriously, considering it works well for the project.

I wanted to create a magazine, different than the usual, were one can find music categorised in different moods of music, from happiness, dark, love and even classic musics. But I wanted each genre to be filtered in their categorised colour, a colour that visually represents the mood on its own; so that each theme, each genre has its own style visually. Meanwhile showcasing top 20 different songs from that mood, the magazine also has articles about some artists coming from the perspected theme. The whole idea process was to look at other magazines like the 'Vogue', 'billboard', 'the rolling stones' magazines and other more, and see how they interpret information and make it look interesting. I also noticed that many magazines, have a follow up of different advertisements from different brands to promote their collections or new merchandise.

The main idea was for me to promote music differently, not just looking at one artist's work or seeing the generic top 10 global music charts, like the 'billboard' company does. So the whole idea of diving the music into the themes was for people to explore what they like in a simple manner, where viewers can easily create their own playlist with the given songs mentioned, or even get to know more cool songs they will like that they have never heard of or simply never knew the name of the songs.

If you would like to see the whole magazine, please do contact me (as I have the physical version printed). Its a 36 page magazine, full of articles, amazing songs to listen and a personal document about myself.
