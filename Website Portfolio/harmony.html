This was another school assignment, and the given brief was to create visual posters for a music, culture and art festival that will be taking place in MFCC, Ta' Qali Park. For this project, I had multiple ideas were I can create on vector softwares or even raster softwares for the quality of the idea I had. Here below are the five ideas I had, were I implemented them digitally to see how they would look finalised. However, the main aim was to make it feel for its right audience, which was families visiting this festival. Some of the ideas I had focused more on just music (were I used a hip-hop dancer), some just on culture (were I used a man giving a speech on stage).. so I was sort of stuck in which to choose. I prefered that the poster would show all of the festical's aspects.

Then I realised that the only generic idea I had was the last one, were I masked an image of a crowd into the text 'Harmony' spreaded across the A4 space. However I didn't really like it due to it looking like modern-grunge. I did like the colours, (Im a big fan of black, white and red) but its not just really speaking for the festival. So I thought of colour mapping it, changing the colours and see what would work.

and BAM! This was the moment were I realised that this was the one. I really liked the blue tones on this poster, it made it look very friendly. Here and there, I wanted to arrange its layout and typefaces to keep it simple, yet effective.
